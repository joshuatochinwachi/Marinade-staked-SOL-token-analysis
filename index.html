mSOL is a liquid staking token on the Solana blockchain, representing staked SOL tokens. It allows users to earn staking rewards while maintaining liquidity for use in decentralized finance (DeFi) applications.​

Some Key Features include:

  • Liquid Staking: Stake SOL and receive mSOL, enabling participation in DeFi activities without locking up assets.​

  • Decentralized Delegation: Marinade delegates staked SOL across multiple validators to enhance network security.​

  • DeFi Integration: mSOL is widely accepted across various DeFi platforms on Solana.​
